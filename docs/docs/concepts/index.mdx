# Concepts 

## Governance

IPT is the governance token for Interest Protocol. Token holders can influence the protocol and lead it after launch by creating and voting on proposals. Governance should be thought as the mechism to institue change in the protocol and token holders as the management of the protocol.  

## USDi

USDi is a stablecoin issued by Interest Protocol and stands for USD with interest. USDi automatically earns compounding interest without requiring holders to stake it anywhere. USDi is created by either depositing USDC or through Borrowing. 1 USDi is redeemable for 1 USDC. 1 USDC deposited mints 1 USDi for the depositor.
